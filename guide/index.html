Guides

Welcome to our comprehensive guides section! Here you'll find detailed instructions for sideloading and using various applications on your iOS device.

Getting Started

Before diving into specific app guides, make sure you have:

  • Enabled Developer Mode on your device
  • Set up a signing service (like DxSign)

Available Guides

Signing Methods

  • Feather - A modern open-source alternative for signing apps
  • Web Signing - Sign apps directly from your web browser
  • ESign - Popular iOS app signing tool

Emulators

  • MeloNX - Play Nintendo Switch games on iOS and iPadOS
  • Dolphin - Play Nintendo Wii and GameCube games on iOS and iPadOS
